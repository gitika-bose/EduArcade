EduArcade is a forum for mathematical empowerment for young children who love games like I did, and admittedly, still do. I founded this website in my junior year in high school after undertaking numerous courses in computer programming that inspired me to begin creating my very own games. The rigorous IB Higher Level Mathematics curriculum showed me the puzzles that mathematics possesses. I wondered why from a young age, children are not given the opportunity to see math as the puzzle I could see before me. So, I created a website that teaches math with the fun element of a game! I designed the website while relentlessly making and rejecting games. After a few came alive on the website, I created a team of programmers. Together, we built a number of games and as the number of games grow, we will continue to expand the website. If you have difficulties loading the games, it may because you are using it on the phone. Otherwise it could take a few minutes to work.

If you enjoyed this website, you may want to visit http://leanvigor.com/. LeanVigor is dedicated to helping high schoolers with their college applications by providing advice based on personal experience.
