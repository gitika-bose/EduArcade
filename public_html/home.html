The Games

A sneak peek into some of the games offered

Basic

We offer exciting games for young children, typically first and second grade students. These will mostly involve the study of numbers and the use of basic operations. This is a good place for students to begin their Mathematical journey!

View Details

Intermediate

Intermediate level games can be challenging but are equally fun! Students can explore larger numbers with more complex functions, along with some basic albegra and geometry problems. Aimed at third and fourth grade students.

View Details

Advanced

Get ready to apply all you have learnt. Here, the games will get more challenging with emphasis on solving complex problems in albegra and geometry. Most ideal for students in the fourth grade or above, or anyone who loves puzzles.
